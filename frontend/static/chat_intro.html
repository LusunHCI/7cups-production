Instructions

In the first part of the experiment, you will engage in a conversation with a chatbot named Andrew. A chatbot is a conversational agent that uses natural language processing and artificial intelligence to communicate with its users. You will serve as a listener to Andrew as he reveals how his recent breakup has been affecting him emotionally. As you listen and interact with Andrew, your goal is to support him through empathy, affirmation, and active listening.

This conversation is intended to simulate a chat on the platform, 7 cups. 7 cups is an online mental health community in which people can register to be members, who have the opportunity to receive mental health support, or to be listeners, who can provide mental health support to the members.

After your chat with Andrew, you will get the opportunity to analyze and improve the flow of your conversation by answering some simple questions in order to refine the design of the chatbot.
